TRANSPORTATION

TRANSPORTATION

Getting around Stockholm is quite easy. The famous Gamla Stan (old town) could be discovered on foot as it is small enough. However, if you want to venture in and around the city center for varied and richer experiences and at the same time to save time public transport is the fastest, cheapest and easiest mode of transportation. With an SL day-card you can have 24 hours unlimited access to trams, buses and trains all over Stockholm.
You can also get around by taxis and cars. We have options to provide you with rental car and even a chauffeur for your comfort and convenience.

MUSEUMS

MUSEUMS

Stockholm has many historical and modern museums, but we offer the best and impressive selections for you to have a lasting museum experience, the Nordic Museum and the ABBA Museum. The first one is the Nordic Museum, based in central Stockholm, dedicated to the cultural history and ethnography of Sweden from the early modern to the contemporary period.
Secondly, the ABBA Museum is a Swedish interactive exhibition about the famous pop band ABBA which showcases the band's collected works in a contemporary, interactive setting.

SKANSEN

SKANSEN

Skansen, opened in 1891, is the first open-air museum and zoo in Sweden which shows the way of life in the different parts of Sweden before the industrial era. The exhibits include a full replica of a 19th century town and the open-air zoo contains a wide range of wild Scandinavian animals as well as some rare breeds of farm animals.
